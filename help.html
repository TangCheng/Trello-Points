Trello Points Chrome Extension Help

Trello Points is a Chrome Extension that adds a notion of points to the card of a Trello board. Each lists display the total points of his cards. In the continuity of Trello.com, you are free to add any meanings to the points of card. It can be:

  • the story points in a scrum board
  • days to complete the task in a todo list board
  • ...
As Trello's board are used for many unuspected things, Trello Points can help you to add another criteria to the card (label, members, votes due date, check list are already provided by Trello).

Adding points to a card

Points are written in between parentheses at the end of the title of a card. upon an error I want to see an awesome 404 page. (5) Upon saving the card, Trello Points picks up the assigned points and shows them as a badge in the lower left corner of the card.

When you flip a card to add more information to that card, you are also presented with a point picker upon editing the title. This allows for easier entry. You can configure wich sequence to use in the point picker in the options of the extension. The commonly used semi-fibonacci sequence: 0, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 20 is used per default.

Allowed entries for points are all numbers, decimals (like .5 or 4.2) and the question mark (?) for unknowns. Negative points are not (yet) supported...

Filtering cards

When you activate some filters (labels, authors...) the total score of each list is updated to take into account only visible cards. You also have the options to display others scores : total filtered vs. total, percentage.. just go to the options of the extension.
